# Dataset processing & training: Sudoku (`main_sudoku.py`) vs TRM (`trm_base/pretrain.py`)

`main_sudoku.py` is a thin wrapper; the Sudoku training pipeline lives in `sudoku/main_sudoku.py` with `core/` and `sudoku/sudoku_tokenizer.py`.

## Transpose, symmetries, and RoPE

- **Transpose (and other symmetries)** are applied only in the TRM data builder when `num_aug > 0` and for augmented copies, not in the HF streaming Sudoku path: `[trm_base/build_sdku_data.py](../trm_base/build_sdku_data.py)` (lines 31–56: `transpose_flag`, row/col permutations, digit map).
- **RoPE** is wired in TRM when `pos_encodings: rope` (default in `trm.yml`): `[trm_base/trm.py](../trm_base/trm.py)` (`RotaryEmbedding`, `seq_info`), `[trm_base/layers.py](../trm_base/layers.py)` (`apply_rotary_pos_emb`, attention). The Sudoku stack uses learned `PosEmbed` in `[core/layers.py](../core/layers.py)` instead.
